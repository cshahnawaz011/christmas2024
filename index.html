Merry Christmas 2024

Celebrate the magic, joy, and wonder of the holiday season!

Why Do We Celebrate Christmas?

Christmas is celebrated to commemorate the birth of Jesus Christ, bringing joy, love, and hope to millions. It’s a time for family, giving, and spreading kindness across the world.

Fun Facts About Christmas

  • The tradition of Christmas trees began in Germany.
  • The first Christmas card was sent in 1843.
  • Santa Claus is based on St. Nicholas, a real-life monk.
